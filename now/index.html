What I Am Doing Now

Distraction Free Life

I remember the exact moment when I observed my mind being absolutely incapable to focus, not able to remember anything – rather disturbing experience.

  • disabled phone notification
  • started to use phone in Greyscale mode most of the time
  • removed messenger application from home screen, checking new messages irregularly
  • stopped using social network
  • stopped reading news on daily basis

Learning Rust, Full Time Without Excuses

Finally, things get serious. Since I’m not using it in work, full time means anytime in my free time devoted for learning new tech stuff.

And I have to say – what a wonderful experience it is. It feels like almost anything I read makes me better developer – which means that either I’m such a sloppy dev or Rust community is doing really great job in making great learning materials.

So definitely recommend reading about it – even if you won’t use the language itself.
