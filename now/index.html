What I Am Doing Now

Controled Cold Exposures

After reading about Wim Hof, it was no-brainer to at least give it a shot. So far I've been doing:

  • cold water immersion – full body, water temperature 10-4 °C, time in water between 1—2 minutes
  • walking bare foot in snow
  • wearing fewer clothes in the winter

Experimenting With Lower Carbohydrates Intake

It was sad moment to realize after following dietary discussion for couple of months that still the best advice regarding one diet is to simply experiment and observe what works for you. Anyway, one particular hype which I've decided to join is restriction of carbs. Rulebook:

  • no processed sugar/junk food
  • minimize consumption of other carbs except after physical activity, replace them with the fats
