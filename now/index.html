What I Am Doing Now

Controled Cold Exposures

After reading about Wim Hof, it was no-brainer to at least give it a shot. So far I've been:

  • doing cold water immersion – full body, water temperature 10-4 °C, time in water between 1—2 minutes
  • walking bare foot in the snow
  • wearing fewer clothes in the winter

Experimenting With Lower Carbohydrates Intake

It was sad moment to realize after following dietary discussion for couple of months that still the best advice regarding one diet is to simply experiment and observe what works for you. Anyway, one particular hype which I've decided to join is restriction of carbs. Rulebook:

  • no processed sugar/junk food
  • minimize consumption of other carbs except after physical activity, replace them with fats

Distraction Free Life

I remember the exact moment when I observed my mind being absolutely incapable to focus, not able to remember anything – rather disturbing experience.

  • disabled phone notification
  • started to use phone in Greyscale mode most of the time
  • removed messanger application from home screen, checking new messages irregularly
  • stopped using social network
  • stopped reading news on daily basis
